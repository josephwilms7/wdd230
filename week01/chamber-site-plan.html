Site Purpose

The Lethbridge Chamber of Commerce acts as medium for individuals, businesses and other locals to learn more about business in their area. This also includes raising awareness of commercial and commerce oppurtunites in the local area.

Scenarios

  • Someone looking for what business are present in the Lethbridge area
  • Someone looking for somewhere to eat or spend time with friends and family
  • Businesses looking for oppurtunites to conenct and collaborate with other businesses
